Other Interests

Outside research, I’m interested in the kinds of things that sharpen attention, deepen perspective, or simply make life feel richer.

Languages

English, Marathi, and Hindi. I also understand some Spanish, though I speak it less fluently — enough to follow more than I can confidently say.

Movement

I enjoy regular gym training, hiking, and the discipline of staying physically active. Strength training has become one of the most grounding parts of my routine.

Books & Ideas

I’m drawn to books that remain morally, politically, or psychologically alive over time. Some favorites include Annihilation of Caste, 1984, and Crime and Punishment — among many others.

Film & Music

I like films and music that stay with me emotionally or aesthetically. A few favorite films include Interstellar, the Before trilogy, 2001: A Space Odyssey, and La La Land.

Travel

I enjoy seeing places through walking, observation, and everyday life rather than only through itineraries. Some of the countries I’ve visited include Argentina, Norway, the Czech Republic, Germany, and Poland.

Cities

I have a special fondness for New York City — its energy, scale, contradictions, and the feeling that every neighborhood carries its own rhythm.
