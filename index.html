Research

My work sits broadly within applied AI. I’m interested in building useful machine learning methods and intelligent systems for technical and structured domains, especially where performance, efficiency, and real-world constraints all matter.

What I’m broadly interested in

I care about machine learning methods that go beyond generic benchmarks and connect with practical problems. Much of my work lives around learning systems, language models, generative approaches, and graph-based methods for complex technical settings.

Applied AI Machine Learning Systems Large Language Models Generative Models Graph Learning Efficient Inference

How I think about it

I’m drawn to work that feels both rigorous and usable — methods that hold up technically, but are also grounded in real applications. I like problems where intelligence, efficiency, and structure all need to be taken seriously at once.
