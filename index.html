Kiran Thorat

Researcher working broadly in applied AI, with interests in machine learning systems, large language models, generative methods, and graph-based learning for structured real-world problems.

I’m especially drawn to work that combines technical depth with practical usefulness — methods that are not only strong in theory, but also meaningful in application.

Research

My work sits broadly within applied AI. I’m interested in building useful machine learning methods and intelligent systems for technical and structured domains, especially where performance, efficiency, and real-world constraints all matter.

Some themes I return to often include large language models, generative approaches, graph-based learning, and computational systems for complex technical problems. You can read more on the research page.

Applied AI Machine Learning Systems Large Language Models Generative Models Graph Learning Efficient Inference

Other Interests

Outside research, I care about language, exercise, books, films, music, travel, and cities. These are not separate from the way I think; they shape perspective, attention, and taste.

I’m especially drawn to things that reveal more depth with time: places that open up slowly, books that stay alive after finishing, and films or songs that linger. I’ll keep more photos and personal material on the other interests page.
