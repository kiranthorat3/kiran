Research

My research interests are broadly in machine learning, intelligent systems, and computational methods. I’m particularly drawn to problems that connect conceptual clarity with practical relevance — work that is not only technically interesting, but also useful, efficient, and grounded in real applications.

Areas I’m interested in

  • Machine learning and AI
  • Large language models
  • Generative methods
  • Graph-based learning
  • Efficient computation
  • Structured and technical problem solving
  • Systems that connect theory and practice

How I think about research

I’m interested in work that balances technical depth with real usefulness. In general, I like research that is elegant in idea, practical in implementation, and capable of helping us understand or build better systems.

I’m especially drawn to problems where computation interacts with structure, complexity, and real-world constraints.
